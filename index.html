Who we Are

We are a grass-roots investment company specializing in lifecycle investing.

We differ from every other RIA in that instead of schmoozing rich clientele, we are currently operating at a loss to do the exact opposite: We are targeting young people who have just started investing, or are looking to get into it.

We stand for simplicity, transparency, and courage. Our aggressive strategy leverages your assets to aim for a 30% return. This higher return is not generated by stock-picking or market timing tactics that are more likely to fail than succeed, but is instead paid for by increasing your risk of loss. This is backed up by a rigorous mathematical theory known as CAPM.

How do we make money?

Currently, we don't. We are operating at a loss. In the near future we plan on charging 1% fee, but for this to generate significant revenue we will first have to grow our assets under managment (AUM) to multiple millions of dollars.

Our incentives are aligned with yours: If you make more money, we make more money.
